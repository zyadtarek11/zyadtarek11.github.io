Food!

In Egypt, there are several popular burger chains and restaurants, but some of them have faced boycotts due to various reasons, including pricing, food quality concerns, or political and social issues. Here are a few notable ones, along with the boycott movements associated with them:

  1. *Fuddruckers* – While popular, Fuddruckers faced some boycotts in Egypt due to its high prices, which led some customers to seek more affordable local alternatives.
  2. *The Cookery* – This Egyptian-based chain, known for gourmet burgers, has also faced occasional boycotts, particularly due to pricing and the perception that the food quality didn’t justify the cost.
  3. *Smoky Burger* – Although generally well-liked, Smoky Burger has seen some calls for boycotts related to issues like pricing, quality, or corporate practices that didn’t align with consumer values.
  4. *Ovio* – This restaurant, offering burgers along with other dishes, has faced some backlash from patrons who feel that the cost of their burgers is too high for the portion size and quality.
  5. *Zapata's* – The Mexican-themed restaurant, known for offering burgers with a twist, has faced boycotts at times due to the perceived lack of consistency in food quality and customer service.
